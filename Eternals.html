ETERNALS FINAL TRAILER



SYNOPSIS

As the Marvel Cinematic Universe epands, witness the first superheroes that walked amongst humankind since the beginning of time called the Eternals. A race of superhumans that helped humankind in any way they could to prevenet rogue Eternals called Deviants from destroying and feeding off of humans. But as time passes by, an unknown threat that could possibly destroy humankind will test the loyalty and relationships amongst the team and whether the Earth is worth saving or not.
