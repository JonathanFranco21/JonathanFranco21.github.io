Werewolf By Night Official Trailer

Synopsis

In the first Disney+ special in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, journey into this horror special that serves as a story that will make you believe in monsters. When the leader of a group of monster hunters mourn the loss of their leader, Ulysses Bloodstone, a challenge arises amongst the group. To retrieve the powerful Bloodstone to become the new leader, the hunters will be forced into a maze to test their skills and their loyalty. But in the meantime, things aren't what they seem amongst the hunters.

Outcome

As a fan of Marvel and as a fan of the Marvel Cinematic Universe and where it's taking it's new heroes, I found Marvel Studios' Special Presentation: Werewolf By Night to be a spectacular Halloween special that not only pays homage to the classic monster movies of the 1930's, the 1940's, the 1950's, and even the early 1960's, but it also makes itself feel separate from the Marvel Cinematic Universe in its entirety! With a story that doesn't require a new viewer to see any older entries in Phase 4 or the Marvel Cinematic Universe as a whole, this special brings a new genre into the ones it's already established.
