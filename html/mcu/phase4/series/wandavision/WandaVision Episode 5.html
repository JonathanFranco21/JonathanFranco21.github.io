EPISODE 5: ON A VERY SPECIAL EPISODE...





SYNOPSIS

Now in the 80's sitcom era, Vision starts to suspect that everything isnt what it seems after finding out about the Maximoff Anomoly. In the real world, more starts to come forward as Hayward sees Wanda more of a threat and SWORD might not be what it seems.

OVERALL CONTENT(CONTENT CONTAINS SPOILERS!!)

  • Wanda and Visionargue amongst each other softly then loudly throughout the episode which starts to become unsettling.

Hayward claims that the surveliance footage from SWORD headquarters shows Wanda stealing Vision's corpse and ressurected him.

Vision uses his powers to snap Norm out of the "80's sitcom reality" in which he says that someone is in his head and they she should be stopped before going back to the current reality they're in. This scene is creepy and unsettling.
