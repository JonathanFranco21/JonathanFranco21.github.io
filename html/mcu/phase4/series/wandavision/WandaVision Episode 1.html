EPISODE 1: LIVE BEFORE A STUDIO AUDIENCE





SYNOPSIS

In the 1950's sitcom era, Wanda and Vision are living in Westview as a newlywed couple, but things go wrong when Vision comes to their home for dinner.

OVERALL CONTENT(CONTENT CONTAINS SPOILERS!!)

VIOLENCE/DISTURBING CONTENT

  • Wanda uses her powers to put plates away but accidentally breaks it when it hits Vision. Some things break or drop in the kitchen and is done out of comedic context.
  • Vision's boss chokes on food and the atmosphere of the scene can be creepy and unsteeling during the dinner scene. Vision uses his powers to help his boss from dying by pulling his food out of his throat by phasing through his body.

SUGGESTIVE CONTENT

  • Wanda and Vision are married in the sitcom world, in which there is some kissing.
  • Some of the suggestive lines comes from Agnes which include, "A young thing like you doesn't have to do much, but it's still fun to set the scene", "How to goose your wife so you don't loose your wife", and "The death rate of signle men is twice that fo married men" and some of the other references come from both Wanda and Vision, which include "To impress the wife" and "To impress the husband".
  • A magazine article is called, "How To Treat Your Husband To keep Your Husband".
  • Wanda and Agnes briefly talk about seduction techniques.
  • The house is lit dimly for an accidental romantic evening mixed up with a business dinner with Vision's boss and wife, in which Wanda accidentally blindly seduces Vision's boss as a part of the mixup.

DRINKING/DRUG/GAMBLING CONTENT

  • Agnes remarks, "The only way Ralph would remember our annivarsary is if there was a beer named June 2nd".
  • Wine is served during the business dinner.
