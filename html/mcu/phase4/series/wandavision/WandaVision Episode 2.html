EPISODE 2: DON'T TOUCH THAT DIAL





SYNOPSIS

In the 1960's sitcom era, Vision and Wanda perform for a talent show in Westview. But Wanda starts to see that there is something strange that is happening in town that she doesn't know how to explain.

OVERALL CONTENT(CONTENT CONTAINS SPOILERS!!)

VIOLENCE/DISTURBING CONTENT

  • Both Wanda and Vision get scared of unknown noises but it ends up being a tree branch hitting a window. This could scare young children, but this scene is played for laughs.
  • The atmosphere changes from sitcom to myserious multipple times, like how something "explodes" and Wanda goes outside to find a toy helicopt (in color as the rest of the world is in black and white) found in her bushes,someone tries to reach to Wanda via radio transmission which makes Dottie scared not knowing who Wanda is and resulting the radio to explode and Dottie crushing the wine glass in her hand, making her bleed (the blood is shown and in color while everything else is in black and white), and a bee keeper comes out of the manhole cover from the street at night, which could scare younger viewers.
  • There are some unsettling lines like, "How do you get blood out of white linen? You do it yourself!", "The devil's in the details, Bev", "That's not the only place he is", and "For the children" (this line is said in an eerie and unsettling manner that might have viewers question the meaning and intenion of the line.
  • Vision jokes about how one of the members of the security committee is a communist.

SUGGESTIVE CONTENT

  • Vision tells Wanda, "Get the lights dear", which referes to them about to make love. Wanda and Vision also flirt with each other.
  • Wanda jokes about her revealing outfit actually being the other part of Vision's outfit for the talent show.
  • Vision says that gum is a form of "masitcation" in which another character misunderstands the word, saying he doesn't do that, which is a joke about self pleasure.
  • Wanda is later seen pregnant at the end of the episode.

DRINKING/DRUG/GAMBLING CONTENT

  • Agnes holds a flask of what is assumed to be alcohol comming "This might help" and also "Really, how is anybody doing this sober?" when in the meeting.
  • Vision accidentally swallows gum after one of the guys pats him on the back, which "gums up" his gears and causes him to act drunk.
